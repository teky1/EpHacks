About

Welcome to the Epsilon Hackathon, hosted by FTC Team #20124! We are a 2nd-year robotics team competing in the FIRST Tech Challenge comprised of 13 students from Centennial High School. Our vision is to help our local community by supporting local businesses, performing outreach, and spreading awareness of FTC and STEM through events and workshops.


This hackathon will feature 15 algorithm-based questions and various challenges relating to programming. This year, the Epsilon Hackathon will take place at the Miller Branch Library in the Avalon Room (9421 Frederick Rd, Ellicott City, MD 21042). Food will be served, and participants have the opportunity to win prizes!
